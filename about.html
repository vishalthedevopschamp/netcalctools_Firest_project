About NetCalcTools

NetCalcTools is a free IP subnet calculator built for network engineers, students, system administrators, and anyone who works with IPv4 or IPv6 addressing. Our goal is simple: give you accurate subnet calculations instantly, without ads getting in the way, without a login wall, and without unnecessary clutter.

Why we built this

Most subnet calculators online are either buried in ads, hidden behind sign-up walls, or built on outdated interfaces that are painful to use on a phone. We wanted a tool that's fast, accurate, accessible, and genuinely pleasant to use — whether you're studying for a CCNA exam, configuring a VLAN, or double-checking a CIDR range at 2 AM.

What you can calculate

NetCalcTools supports IPv4 subnetting (network address, broadcast address, usable host range, subnet mask, wildcard mask, CIDR notation) and IPv6 subnetting (network prefix, address range, total addresses). A full IPv4 subnet reference table is also available for quick lookups from /1 to /32.

Accuracy

All calculations run entirely in your browser using standard bitwise arithmetic — no data is sent to a server, and no IP address you enter is ever logged or stored.
